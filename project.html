Virtual Tour

This project was developed using Panorago, and I successfully created virtual tours for various schools in the Sidoarjo and Surabaya areas. The tools I used to create these virtual tours included a Samsung 360 camera, a smartphone, a tripod, and a laptop. Additionally, I utilized Caesium and Adobe Photoshop as supplementary tools to enhance the quality and optimize the images used in the virtual tours.

WiX Site

I utilize Wix to create professional and visually appealing websites for micro-businesses. These websites aim to showcase the products of micro-business owners, ensuring their satisfaction while attracting potential customers. Additionally, I have successfully designed Wix websites for schools, specifically highlighting the unique qualities of a kindergarten. Moreover, I have developed profile portfolios for individuals as a means of identification and representation. Lastly, I have created website pages for motor showrooms, catering to the needs of sales personnel in showcasing their products effectively.
